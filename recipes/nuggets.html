Odin's Chicken Nuggets Recipe

The best homemade Chicken Nuggets (no oil)

For this recipe we will bake the nuggets using the oven, not frying them into seed oil.

Ingredients:

  • Minced chicken
  • Corn flakes
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Open the meat's box
  2. Add the corn flakes and the garlic
  3. Bake them at 250 degrees
